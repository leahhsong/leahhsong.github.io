Who is Leah?

I'm currently a three-year student at Northfield Mount Hermon. I define myself as a student-athlete and a passionate writer. At NMH, I play JV soccer in the fall and I am part of the varsity ice hockey team in the winter. I started playing ice hockey at the age of eight. Since then, hockey has been in part of my life for a very long time, as my family loved playing winter sports. Other than ice hockey, me and my family enjoyed skiing together. I also skied competitively like I played hockey. However, my left leg got fractured after an incident. Furthermore, I'm part of Lamplighter, a student newspaper club at NMH. At Lamplighter, I have been publishing different articles over the past two years at NMH that you can check out in the spotlight of my recent works down below.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, writing, and spending time with my friends and family. One of the most memorable books I read is Atomic Habits by James Clear. I also love exploring new places and cultures, which has inspired many of my writings and led me to become a more creative thinker!
